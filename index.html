Creating the Pisay Online Community

Our Goal

This organization aims to unite all PSHS Campuses in the Philippines to promote and create a healthy and student- friendly online environment for the Pisay Community.

Our Plan

We communicate with each campus through representatives to update the recent state of each respective campus' online community. A workforce of students and teachers from various regions in the Philippines coordinate with each other to develop strategic plans to improve the quality of each online communities and to fix any problem, may it be online or in real-life, caused by the student's use of social media and the like. Events such as webinars, tournaments, etc. will be held regularly, featuring experts from various fields aiming to inspire Pisay students to contribute to the cause.
